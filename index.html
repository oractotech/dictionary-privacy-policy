Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Dictionary app developed by Oracto Tech.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal user information. It is fully offline and uses a local database for translated word lookup.

Permissions

The app uses the RECORD_AUDIO permission only to support voice input functionality for searching English words. This audio input is used locally on the device and is not stored or transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services or SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children. As no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all age groups.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
